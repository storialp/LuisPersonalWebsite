We build disruptive ventures. Here is our recipe

Up to 40 participants

We will select the 40 most brilliant minds to join Vento and create amazing teams. You will be free to choose your own co-founders, and we will provide you with our support and experience to make sure you find the best match.

Real needs to start from

Leading companies and startups have shared with us their direct experience in the market. This way we will make sure that each team will have the possibility to start from a validated problem.

5-months full-time program

Each participant will go through a journey of team building, research, design, testing, and implementation. Get ready for some crazy fast-paced rollercoaster of emotions, and then at the end of the ride your startup will take off.
